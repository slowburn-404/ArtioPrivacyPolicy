Privacy Policy for Artio

Effective Date: 27/08/2024

At Artio, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Artio:

  • Personal Information: We collect your email address for authentication purposes through Firebase.
  • Sketch Data: We store the sketches you create for cloud synchronization, allowing access across devices.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app to improve its functionality and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and provide access through Firebase.
  • Data Sync: To securely store and synchronize sketches across devices.
  • App Functionality: To maintain real-time collaboration and app security via internet connection.
  • Improvements: To monitor performance, fix bugs, and improve the user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your information, except with service providers like Firebase for essential app functions, and when required by law.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure protocols to protect your data, though no method is 100% secure.

5. User Control

You have the right to access, update, and delete your account and data by contacting us.

6. Internet Permissions

Artio uses internet access for synchronization, collaboration, and authentication purposes.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of Artio constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at datahive07@gmail.com.
